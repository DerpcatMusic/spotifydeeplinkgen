Spotify Deep Linking Explained:

Spotify deep linking creates direct links that take listeners straight to your content on Spotify, skipping the search. It's a direct line to your song, and even better, it can put your track inside your playlist so when the song finishes, the next track kicks in!

Why it's Awesome: Deep linking gets your music heard, keeps people listening, and gives you control over the listening experience.
